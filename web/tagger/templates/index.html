CLMR Music Tagger

Online interface of Contrastive Learning of Musical Representations by J. Spijkervet and J.A. Burgoyne (2020).

We pre-train a using a task-agnostic, self-supervised objective on unlabeled, raw (musical) audio data.

We then fine-tune a linear classifier on the task of automatic music tagging and evaluate the performance.

Despite unsupervised, contrastive pre-training and fine-tuning on the music classification task using linear classifier, we achieve state-of-the-art results on the MTAT dataset relative to fully supervised training.
